what's a paperless rent agreement?

A paperless rent agreement is a digital version of a traditional rental contract that can be created, signed, and stored entirely online, without the need for physical paperwork. It typically includes all the terms and conditions of a rental agreement, such as the rental amount, lease duration, security deposit, and responsibilities of both the landlord and tenant.
